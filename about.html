About WanderLux Travel

Our Story

Established in 2025, WanderLux Travel Agency was founded with the goal to create personalised and unique experiences to explore the world. We believe travel enriches lives, broadens perspectives, and creates lasting memories.

Our team of travel consultants has explored various destinations and uses their firsthand experiences to create personalised itineraries according to your preferences.

To provide exceptional travel planning services that fulfill your dreams, offering personalised experiences tailored to each customer's unique preferences and budget.
